ABOUT & HISTORY

Heart's Delight Coffee House was founded by Cyrille Thomas Yasashiro on January 2023 with the goal of being a bridge between two relationships. Cyrille was inspired to start the coffee shop after observing how a simple cup of coffee or tea could bring people together and facilitate meaningful conversations. He wanted to create a cozy and welcoming space where people could come together and connect over a warm beverage.

Since its founding, Heart's Delight Coffee House has become a beloved destination for locals and visitors alike. Our wide selection of handcrafted beverages, made with the finest ingredients, has gained a reputation for excellence. We are also known for our fresh pastries, sandwiches, and other light fare, as well as our unique feature where customers can request any dessert from around the world.
